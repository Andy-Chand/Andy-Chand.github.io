Andy Chand - GIS Specialist

I am a GIS professional with over 2 years of experience in the field. Prior to my current role, I spent over 5 years in food quality control, which has provided me with a strong background and unique skill set.

My expertise includes using GIS software and tools to analyze and interpret data, create maps, and perform spatial analysis. I have a thorough understanding of geospatial databases, data collection techniques, and data visualization techniques, which I have used to support decision-making in food quality control.

In my previous role, I was responsible for ensuring that food products met the highest standards of quality. I worked closely with quality control, research and development, and production departments to identify areas of concern and develop solutions to improve food quality. This experience has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of the food industry and the importance of data analysis in making informed decisions.

I am a highly motivated and enthusiastic professional who is always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow. My passion for GIS, combined with my technical skills and experience in food quality control, make me a valuable asset to any organization. I am eager to bring my skills and expertise to a new role and continue to make a positive impact in the field.
