Andy Chand - GIS Specialist

I am a dedicated GIS professional with fours years of experience, specializing in the management and analysis of geospatial data. My expertise lies in utilizing tools such as ArcGIS Pro, ArcMap, ArcGIS Online, and other GIS platforms to create detailed maps, conduct spatial analyses, and manage large geodatabases. My work has consistently focused on optimizing workflows, automating processes through Python scripting, and enhancing data integrity in GIS operations.

In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong background in data analysis. I have conducted in-depth analyses of large datasets, such as municipal recycling programs, leading to actionable insights that have improved program efficiency and reporting standards. My experience includes developing and implementing processes to analyze complex datasets, allowing for the identification of patterns and the formulation of strategies to enhance outcomes.

I am also skilled in managing and optimizing SQL databases, where I ensure that data is accurately integrated and georeferenced within GIS applications. This includes performing regular updates, executing complex SQL queries, and maintaining the overall performance of the databases. My ability to manage these databases effectively has been crucial in supporting various GIS projects.

Driven by a passion for GIS and data management, I am continually seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and contribute to impactful projects. My background equips me with the skills necessary to tackle complex geospatial challenges and deliver solutions that enhance decision-making processes. I am eager to bring my expertise to new projects and continue making a positive impact in the field.
