Andy Chand - GIS Specialist

I have a background in quality assurance and technical experience in GIS. I am passionate about using my knowledge in both of these fields to come up with creative solutions to geospatial problems. This includes finding solutions through coding, interpreting drawings and analyzing figures to showcase information to help clients, inform decisions, and to achieve results.

My experience in the GIS field have allowed me to develop GIS-specific skills, including:

  • Updating and handling GIS data to produce maps and figures,
  • Learning various programming languages (Python, FME, SQL, R, C#, HTML, and Javascript),
  • Experience using CAD software (AutoCAD, Civil3D),
  • Managing, updating, and modifying databases, and
  • Developing programs to handle and showcase data from various source.

Through my previous years of work experience in the QA/QC field and as a supervisor, I developed numerous transferrable skills to apply to a GIS Specialist role. These transferable skills include:

  • Project management skills,
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills,
  • Strong organizational and time management skills,
  • Attention to detail,
  • Auditing data and reports,
  • The ability to work independently and within a team environment,
  • Leading teams, and
  • Self-motivated and problem-solving abilities.
